FAQ's

  • Who can use the Dhanda App?

    Dhanda is made for empowering freelancers, micro enterprises, small and mid-sized businesses - all alike. You could use it across for any business type.

  • Do you charge any commissions?

    Absolutely not. You do not pay any transaction or commission charge on the orders received via our platform.

  • Where can I download it from?

    The application is available for free and can be downloaded via the Play Store. The IOS version would be available soon too.

  • Is it available in regional languages?

    Not yet, but we are in process to launch in over 14 Indian regional languages in the coming months.

  • Is it secure?

    Don't worry! All your data is encrypted and 100% secured. All data is stored in cloud servers in India.
